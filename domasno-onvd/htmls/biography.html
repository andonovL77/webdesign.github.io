Luka Andonov - Developer | Designer | Innovator

A picture from Ohrid

Hello! , I come originally from the beautiful country of Macedonia and currently residing in the vibrant city of Stip. I'm pursuing my passion for technology and innovation by studying Software Engineering at Finki in Skopje, where I'm currently in my first year.

Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by the world of computers and software. My journey into the realm of coding began with HTML and CSS,

As I delve deeper into my studies, I'm excited to explore various programming languages and technologies to become a well-rounded software engineer.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring the rich cultural heritage of Macedonia, trying out new foods, and spending time in nature. I believe in the power of continuous learning and growth, both personally and professionally, and I'm eager to see where my journey in software engineering takes me.
